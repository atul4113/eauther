Why?

Tablets and smartphones are becoming natural
learning devices for pupils and students worldwide,

mAuthor allows to create content once and have it
immediately ready for all of them.

What is it?

mAuthor is an on-line, professional service
to create OS and screen resolution
agnostic, interactive, educational content
that can be exported to any SCORM
compliant Learning Platform or as an
off-line package. It can also be instantly
embedded in any website directly from
mAuthor.

Is it for you?

mAuthor is designed for publishing houses or
development companies willing to create
educational interactive content that can be
accessed from Macs, PCs, tablets and
smartphones (iOS, Android).

How to make most of it?

Go through the below demo presentation to get familiar with the numerous
functionalities of the Multiplatform ePublishing Solution.

mAuthor offers:

Lowering your cost by creating content once and making it available for all kinds of devices and screen resolutions.
Steeping your digital publishing learning curve due to intuitive, WYSIWYG and template approach content editing model.
Independence and didactical flexibility due to the open, addon oriented architecture to create various interaction types supporting truly personalized learning experience.
Ability to create rich, interactive content accessible from all kinds of desktop and mobile devices, taking into account touch screen operational specifics.
On-line environment allowing you to outsource the development but still controlling the process and format of the final product as well as enabling to continue and re-use the content after you change the developer.
