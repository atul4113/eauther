mSkills

Publisher - Learnetic SA

Drill and Practice courses for grammar and Vocabulary. mSkills are dedicated to students aged 16+ who are not searching for comprehensive language learning products but want to practise selected areas of language e.g. vocabulary or grammar. The presentation and practice material cover levels from A1 to C2, in British English, offering extensive grammar and vocabulary practice. The highly interactive content, useful vocabulary, grammar and pronunciation practice used in context teamed with the attractive design help make learning engaging and relevant.

Math for Elementary School

Publisher - Learnetic SA

Award winning math for Elementary School is an excellent mathematical program written by Finnish authors. Its Finnish origin provides an excellent pedagogical foundation and provides a successful teaching methodology proven world-wide. Although it was originally created in Finland, the matching of the content to the new US Common Core Standards for Mathematics is extraordinarily high. The program focuses on foundational understanding of number and operational skill mastery in the same way as the new Common Core State Standards.

Reflex’Deutsch Grammatik + Wortschatz

Publisher - COMMEST MULTIMEDIA SAS

Reflex'Deutsch Grammatik and Reflex’Deutsch Wortschatz is a series of digital resources dedicated to the revision, consolidation and intensive learning of German grammar and vocabulary. Both Reflex'Deutsch Grammatik and Reflex’Deutsch Wortschatz consist of 60 lessons, divided into three resources covering levels from A1 to C2. A multitude of exercises allow students to evaluate their level, to revise, consolidate and test their knowledge of German grammar and vocabulary. Reflex'Deutsch Grammatik and Reflex’Deutsch Wortschatz is the ideal tool to enrich the courses, to allow students to work grammatical and lexical themes covered in class and to prepare for exams in German.
